Crater Foundation - Marketing Poster

The Crater Foundation is a nonprofit organization that was established in the year 1992 to aid graduating students in District 6. The foundation provides scholarship assistance to all graduating Seniors at Crater High School who apply for one. They also provide scholarship assistance to students continuing their education (after completing at least one year of college), provide financial aid to students and families in need, supply funds to purchase necessary items such as coats, shoes, school supplies, and classroom materials. The Crater Foundation also helps out by funding special building projects and athletic facility improvements. The Crater Foundation has awarded over $3.8 million in scholarships to Crater High graduates, which is absolutely incredible for both them and all of the students they have helped. During the first trimester of our first year in CSB at Crater High School, our class was assigned with producing a marketing poster for The Crater Foundation. I enjoyed doing this assignment because not only did I get to learn more about photo editing, but I also got to learn a lot about the organization that I was creating the poster for.
