Outliers - All Steps - Final - Infographic

After reading the book, Outliers, and creating all five infographics for the different topics discussed in it, our class was tasked with producing a grand final piece, putting together all of our infographics into one huge, beautiful display. The topics ranged from The Matthew Effect and the ten thousand hour rule, to taking advantage of opportunities and understanding the effects that cultural legacies can have in certain situations. I had at first struggled to create a certain style for each of my infographics since I am not very creative, but I got the hang of it all once I had some time to think about what I was really going to do for my particular take on the whole assignment. I really enjoyed putting together this gigantic poster because of the extra, valuable experience it gave me with building an extremely high resolution picture and expertly crafting it to my liking with every small tweak I had made overtime with it. I chose this graphic and displayed it as the first on my showcase page because I was really proud of the quality and the amount of content I had put into the whole thing in the end. Not only did I just put all of the infographics onto one piece, but I also had to create a nice title and attractive borders around each infographic.

Aquaponics - Science - Lab Report

Near the end of my sophomore year at Crater High School, our science teacher had assigned the class a massive aquaponics project. This is most likely the biggest project I have constructed in Crater High School so far. I had to include everything I had generated in this assignment, from the ideas for the blueprint of the aquaponics system I had discovered online, to each and every piece of the poster we were required to make for our system, including various pictures and information on how we had completed the whole entire project. I very much enjoyed working on this aquaponics project because I got to work along with a team (but as always I did a lot of the work). Surprisingly, our aquaponics system had won the best in the entire class and was the one used to conduct all of the rest of our experiments on and collect all of our data that we would need in order to complete the rest of the project for our final display. I chose this piece as my first segment of writing because it shows that I can still work well with a team and put a lot of hard work into what I am doing when I am focused, driven, and motivated to do so.

Crater Foundation - Marketing Poster

The Crater Foundation is a nonprofit organization that was established in the year 1992 to aid graduating students in District 6. The foundation provides scholarship assistance to all graduating Seniors at Crater High School who apply for one. They also provide scholarship assistance to students continuing their education (after completing at least one year of college), provide financial aid to students and families in need, supply funds to purchase necessary items such as coats, shoes, school supplies, and classroom materials. The Crater Foundation also helps out by funding special building projects and athletic facility improvements. The Crater Foundation has awarded over $3.8 million in scholarships to Crater High graduates, which is absolutely incredible for both them and all of the students they have helped. During the first trimester of our first year in CSB at Crater High School, our class was assigned with producing a marketing poster for The Crater Foundation. I enjoyed doing this assignment because not only did I get to learn more about photo editing, but I also got to learn a lot about the organization that I was creating the poster for.

Cambiar El Mundo - Spanish - Infographic

In Spanish 2, we were tasked with constructing an infographic that had to do with the past things that we had been doing both inside and outside of the class. We were “changing the world in 30 days” by doing different tasks that rendered positivity each and every day, from opening the door for people and giving out compliments, to donating food, clothing, and other important things. For this infographic assignment, we had to choose one of the tasks that we had done and talk about how it changes the world and why it is important to do that certain thing. I decided to go a bit more broad with my task because it is much easier for people to do it even if they do not want to put much effort into it at all. I wrote about and showed how smiling can change the world by altering people’s moods and just having a much more positive experience in life with others. Smiling can always make a difference and it is just so easy to do it. This is why I chose the topic of something simple, such as smiling. I wanted to showcase this Spanish infographic because it can both show my spanish skills and my graphic creating skills. It also shows how I can do many different styles of graphics for posters, flyers, infographics, etc. I had a great time exploring the different ways to go about making it and how I had finished it all up in the end.

Business Marketing Plan - Intro To Marketing

In my sophomore class, Intro To Marketing, we were tasked with the biggest assignment we would be getting in the class - creating a marketing plan for our own, made up business. Now, this was quite a tough assignment since I was not really that well associated with how businesses operated and how a marketing plan was done, but throughout the class and the long days, our group pulled through and created a great marketing plan for our fake, little silly business that had to do with cooking as chefs. I really quite enjoyed putting together this tremendous marketing plan because I had to learn a lot to get through the whole assignment and work my hardest to get the best amount of content and quality of content I could. We also made an outstanding slideshow that I had put a lot of work into and looked at every detail for it. This assignment shows that I am quite a perfectionist and I can learn new things quickly to get the job done however I can. Exploring all of these important business and marketing pieces really expanded our knowledge on how the real, actual world works, and that is amazing.
